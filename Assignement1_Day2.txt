1. Start
2. Prepare withdrawing a sum of money at an ATM
	2.1 Insert a card into the designated card slot
	2.2 Fill out the 6-digits-pin
	2.3 Select "Withdraw"
	2.4 Select the amount, or, type the amount you want to withdraw
	2.5 Select "Yes" if you want the receipt, otherwise, select "No"
	2.6 Take out your card from the card slot
	2.7 Take out your cash from the cash slot
3. End
